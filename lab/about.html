Hello, I'm Chelsea James and I'm from New York City. Growing up in New York City was more than I could've ever asked for. I was able to express myself in any way that I wanted.

I was always interested in the arts. I took a chance and auditioned for Laguardia Arts with my minimal art background and I was accepted. My love for art grew tremendously because of the environment that I was exposed to.

My favorite things to do are find new restuarants to go to, find new coffee shops/bakeries to go to, binge watch Netflix, and learn new languages. Since I am from a huge city, I also enjoy strolling around the city to find these new places. Once I find a great new place I bring all my friends and we would hang out there.
